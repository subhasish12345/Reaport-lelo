Format Reference
CHAPTER:
Starts a new chapter. Chapter 1 also starts page numbering.
CHAPTER: Chapter 1 Introduction
HEADING:
Section heading (e.g. 1.1, 1.2 ...)
HEADING: 1.1 Background
SUBHEADING:
Sub-section heading (e.g. 1.1.1)
SUBHEADING: 1.1.1 Technical Feasibility
PARA:
A body paragraph. Uses justify alignment, Times New Roman 12pt, 1.5 line spacing.
PARA: Your paragraph text goes here on a single line.
FIGURE:
Figure caption. A half-page blank space is reserved above for your image. Caption appears in the List of Figures.
FIGURE: Figure 1.1 — System Architecture Diagram
📌 Rules:
• Each tag must be on its own line
• Blank lines between tags are ignored
• Everything before Chapter 1 is TOC / LOF
• TOC & LOF update automatically in Word (Ctrl+A → F9)
